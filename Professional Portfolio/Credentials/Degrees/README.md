- **Cybersecurity** — Management of Security Operations

## M.S. Family & Consumer Sciences (General) — University of Tennessee at Martin (2018)
## M.S.Ed. Teaching-Interdisciplinary — University of Tennessee at Martin (2020)

These two degrees were completed together as a de facto graduate-level psychology curriculum. UTM lacked an M.S. Psychology program; coursework was distributed across FCS and Education departments but maps directly to standard psychology graduate domains.

**Domains covered:**
- **Developmental Psychology** — Theories in Child & Family Studies, Growth & Development School Age Students, Early Childhood Growth & Development, History & Theories of Early Childhood Education
- **Developmental Psychopathology** — Children with Exceptionalities
- **Clinical Neurodevelopmental Psychology** — Introduction to Autism Spectrum Disorder
- **Applied Behavior Analysis** — Behavior Strategies & Interventions
- **Educational Psychology** — Strategies for Differentiated Inclusive Classrooms, Principles & Practice of Effective Teaching
- **Health Psychology / Lifespan Development** — Perspectives on Death & Dying
- **Cognitive Psychology of Language** — Foundations of Literacy Development
- **Quantitative Research Methods** — Educational Statistics, Research Methods FCS, Research in Education
